Our Humble Background

ST SUSAN HIGH SCHOOL is a private founded school found in buyanja sub-couty , rukungiri district along rukungiri- ntungamo road. The school started in 2009 with 200 students

Our commitment and recognition as leaders in Basic education are evident from the various trophies that we have been awarded. With this in mind, we offer an extensive co-curricular program that involves a wide range of activities to ensure a holistic approach towards education.

We are proud of our heritage and grateful for your consideration to entrust us with the education of your child. It is an honour to serve as the principal of Parklands Baptist School, and I warmly invite you to our school.
